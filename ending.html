The comments are still there.
The numbers keep climbing.
But he doesn't refresh the page.
Instead, he opens a new document
and starts writing.
For the next person.
Reading their name in a hashtag
for the first time.
Karrar kept making videos.
The internet moved on.
He didn't forget.
But he kept going anyway.
...
You reached the end.
Thank you for walking with him.
